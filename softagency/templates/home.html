Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity and scope. A simple website may take 4-6 weeks, while a complex custom application could take 3-6 months. During our initial consultation, we'll provide a detailed timeline for your specific project.

Do you provide ongoing support after project completion?

Yes, we offer various support and maintenance packages to ensure your software continues to perform optimally. Our packages include regular updates, security monitoring, bug fixes, and technical support.

What technologies do you work with?

We work with a wide range of technologies including JavaScript (React, Angular, Vue), Python (Django, Flask), PHP (Laravel), mobile development (React Native, Flutter, Swift, Kotlin), and various databases and cloud platforms. We choose the best technology stack for each project's specific requirements.

Can you work with our existing team?

Absolutely! We often collaborate with in-house teams, either by augmenting your existing resources or working alongside your team. We're flexible in our approach to ensure seamless collaboration.

What is your pricing model?

We offer flexible pricing models including fixed-price projects for well-defined scope, time and materials for ongoing work, and dedicated team models for long-term partnerships. We'll recommend the best approach based on your project needs.
